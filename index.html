Advanced Redundancy & Survival

* Full redundancy in flight controls, electronics, and batteries: Redundancy designed into all critical components (engines, Aileron controls, navigation, etc.).

If a component fails, the UAV is programed to fly home (or to any other programmed location) – avoiding the loss of the UAV and its payload. No other UAV has our level of critical system redundancy.

* Fail-safe "Return-to-Base" logic if critical components fail. Includes return-to-base (in the event of fault notice), and auto-commanded go-around on landing approaches.

* Eight industrial-grade servos; each control surface is divided and independently controlled.
